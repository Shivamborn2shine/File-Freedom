Why CloudDrop?

⚡

Lightning Fast

Files upload directly to AWS S3 via presigned URLs. No middleman, no bottlenecks — just raw cloud speed.

🔒

Secure by Default

All files are encrypted at rest and in transit. Share links use unique random codes that can't be guessed.

🌍

Global CDN

CloudFront delivers your shared content from edge locations worldwide for instant access from anywhere.

⏰

Auto-Expiry

Set files to expire after 1 hour, 24 hours, 7 days, or keep them forever. You're in control.

📱

Mobile Friendly

Responsive design works beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops. Share from anywhere.

💸

Serverless = Free Tier

Built on AWS Lambda, S3, and DynamoDB — runs entirely on Free Tier for development and light usage.
